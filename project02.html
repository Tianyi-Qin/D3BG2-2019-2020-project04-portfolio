What we need to do in this class is:

Choose a typography containing at least 5 different weights (no italics, no capital)

Create a typographic gray gradient (different type of grease) from left to right.

Justify the columns by applying the data.

Finally make 3 posters respecting the rule of 3 thirds and 3 others respecting the golden rule.
