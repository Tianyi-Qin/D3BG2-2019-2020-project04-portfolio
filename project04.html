In this project, we designed a 120x176cm poster for a rhum in Brazil.

Promote this alcohol, much less known in France than the rums of the Antilles and which offers an alternative to the latter for making cocktails. Be inspired by positive images associated with Brazil, but respecting Evin law. Renew the existing creation.
