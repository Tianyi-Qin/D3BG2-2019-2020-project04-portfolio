Typography is a language, former poster designers used it to inform, alert or attract attention. It's up to us to give pride of place to words by sublimating them with ours composition and ours graphic choices.

We quickly choose a short text that you like (novel, poetry, song), that touches you or that speaks to us (free time). we establish a word cloud that can then help us create our visual. We draw outlines, visual dynamics.

I chose Tagore's 《Flying Birds collection》 and chose to express the artistic conception of poetry in a natural and casual way.
