We collaborate in a four-person team to develop rules for font design. This set of rules must have continuity, and some theory is needed to verify the rationality of the rules. Finally, through such a rule, we designed a set of fonts. Our rules:

  • All letters are within the circle.
  • The grid is a circle divided equally into 26 parts.
  • The letter consists of an internal grid and an external grid.
